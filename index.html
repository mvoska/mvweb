ABOUT ME

About me

I'm currently an undergrad at Northeastern University in Boston studying computer engineering and entrepreneurship. Outside academics, I'm involved with the NU Entrepreneurs Club as Director of the Husky Startup Challenge, I'm an active member of IEEE, Silver Wings, and Civil Air Patrol. I'm engaged with the entrepreneurial community of Boston and regularly attend startup events. I am a passionate follower of the tech industry and aviation.

My vision

I want to advance the aviation industry through new technologies, making it more accessible for anyone who has dreampt of slipping the surly bonds of Earth.

My experience

I started my first company, Voska Electronics, in 2005, and have since grown it and several other ventures including GigaStorm: a mobile application and website development firm that I founded with my friends and partners, Aakash Patel and Andrew Mass. I learned the necessary skills for running a business as I went along, then taught the process and skills necessary to start a business by directing the Husky Startup Challenge, coaching startups like FilterLight, Genius.box and KrystalBoard.

WHAT I DO

Entrepreneurship

As an owner of two businesses, a board member for the NU Entrepreneurs Club, and an IDEA Coach, I live and breathe startups. I enjoy giving and receiving feedback on ventures and ideas.

ComputerEngineering

At Northeastern University, I am a computer engineering major and a tech enthusiast. I closely follow the tech market and am fascinated by new hardware and software breakthroughs.

Fly Planes

I obtained my private pilot license (Airplane Single Engine Land) in September of 2011 and have logged 75+ hours since, flying mainly Cessna 152 and 172's based at Westosha Airport (5K6) in Wilmot, WI.

Leadership Development

The ability to persuade and motivate people has alway sparked my interest and I strive to improve my leadership through Civil Air Patrol, Air Force ROTC, and Silver Wings.

SIDE PROJECTS

Voska Electronics

Founding Voska Electronics in 2005, it has grown from just a neighborhood business to providing tech support, consulting, and systems engineering services to homes and businesses in the greater Chicagoland and Metro Boston areas. To date it has serviced hundreds of clients.

Silver Wings

During my time in Air Force ROTC, I was a member of Arnold Air Society. I wanted to be able to continue with those values when I left, so I am founding a Silver Wings Chapter at Air Force Detachment 355 to support the community and to help grow civilians and cadets as leaders.

IDEA Coach

Beginning in the 2013 Fall Semester, I will be a specialty coach for Northeastern University's Venture Accelerator, IDEA. As a specialty coach, I will provide technical and business advice and guidance to the startups going through IDEA to help them reach the next stage of their business.

Civil Air Patrol

I am an active member of the Civil Air Patrol Cadet Program at Illinois Lake in the Hills Squadron 282, where we offer leadership development, aerospace education, and emergency services to the region. I'm currently at the rank of Cadet Chief Master Sergeant.
